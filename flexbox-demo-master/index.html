Sarah Molano

New York City-based writer and journalist.

Hey, I'm Sarah! I received my bachelor's degree in English with concentrations in rhetoric and global culture from Binghamton University. After holding various leadership positions on the undergraduate newspaper, I decided to pursue my master's degree in journalism at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism.

My main journalistic interests are housing, policing and prisons and right-wing violence. I hope to work in independent news media, free from the influence of corporate interests.
